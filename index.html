Olivier I. Cornes

My name is Olivier Cornes. I am an aerospace engineer and an expert in computational design. After researching logistic-based design optimization algorithms for NASA at MIT’s engineering systems Laboratory, I joined Aurora Flight Science’s as a conceptual aircraft designer for commercial electric vertical lift (eVTOL) projects. Since then, my interest has focused on researching computational design methods for safety-critical systems. Currently, I am a combat aircraft systems engineer at the Swiss Airforce’s industrial arm, working on projects related to safety-critical avionics and military aircraft certification.
Note: This research is not linked to my employer.

Recently, my research has been focused on applying Natural Language Processing to computational design of safety-critical systems.
